About the Beautiful Game

The beautiful game as we all know it lays its origins back in the early 19th century. Although there were many different versions of the game spread among various different countries, the origins of the game, however, come from England.

Fast forward a bit to the mid-19th century where we find that football branched itself into two main categories: Futsal and Beach football.

Futsal is an indoor version of soccer and can be played on indoor turf or a regular high-school gym floor.

Beach football is just like regular football but except it's played in the sand

The World Cup... An absolute cracking competition that's even better than the Champions League and MUCH MUCH harder to win. If the Champions League is played with teams across Europe than what would be the format for this ?That's right. Countries. The World Cup, played every 4 years, is played amongst countries and football players are called up to their countries World Cup squad (a total of 26 players) based on the country of birth. Various countries go head to head in a 48 'team' format, where there are 12 pots with 4 teams each and the top 3 teams from each pot advance into group stages who then advance into the quarter finals, semi finals, and finally the final. The competition was first introduced 1930 and was first hosted in Uruguay where the host country actually ended up winning the competition. The country with the most World Cup wins in history is Brazil with a total of 5 wins. Fun fact, there are only 8 countries ever to win the World Cup
