Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is known for its sleek, modern design and user-friendly interface.

  • Contrast: Uses minimal colors with ample white space to highlight products effectively.
  • Repetition: Consistent font, layout, and branding reinforce Apple's aesthetic.
  • Alignment: All elements are cleanly aligned, creating a structured layout.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped logically, making navigation intuitive.

Ling's Cars

Ling's Cars is an extremely cluttered and chaotic car leasing website.

  • Contrast: Overuses clashing colors and flashing elements, creating an overwhelming experience.
  • Repetition: Lacks consistency in fonts, colors, and layout, making the design feel erratic.
  • Alignment: Poorly aligned elements create a chaotic and unstructured appearance.
  • Proximity: Overloads the page with too much content without logical grouping, making it hard to navigate.
