About CodeMentor JS

Our mission to make JavaScript learning accessible and engaging

Our Story

CodeMentor JS was created by UNCC computer science students who recognized the challenges beginners face when learning JavaScript. We designed this platform to provide a structured, interactive approach to mastering JavaScript fundamentals.

Our Approach

We believe in learning by doing. Our platform combines concise explanations with hands-on coding exercises that run directly in your browser, providing immediate feedback to reinforce your understanding.
